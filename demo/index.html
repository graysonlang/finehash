FineHash — side-by-side comparison

FineHash is a small TypeScript/JavaScript library that turns an image into a placeholder hash. Source and format details on GitHub.

The columns below mirror the ThumbHash demo — ThumbHash, BlurHash, and a "Teeny" WebP (an uber-compressed WebP) — with FineHash added alongside. Drag/drop or Upload your own images to compare.

(Note: The WebP column is omitted on WebKit-based browsers such as Safari, which do not support WebP encoding via canvas.toDataURL).
